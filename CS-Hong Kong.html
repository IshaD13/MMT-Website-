Hong Kong MMT Case Study

Hong Kong Case Study

There are also case studies which support the use of Methadone, such as one in Hong Kong. Hong Kong has had an MMT program since 1972, stater in response of increased drug use, but recently also has assisted in controlling the heroin epidemic. The cost of the treatment there is extremely cheap at only HK $1 (12 cents) per clinic appointment and is open from early morning to late night seven days per week, which makes it available to a much larger portion of the people. Patients might also feel safe going there as there is a non-judgemental approach and a ''low threshold'' model, meaning that there are few conditions a patient needs to meet to begin treatment. The clinic has acquired great success and research has shown that the patients attending the clinic were showing less risk of injecting and HIV risk behaviours (Geneva: World Health Organization).
